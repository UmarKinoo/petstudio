Level 2 City & Guilds for Dog Grooming Assistants

24 Day Course

Fed up of working for someone else? Don’t want to do shift work or weekends? Looking to achieve a better work/life balance? Want to earn more money?

Designed for those with no experience in dog grooming but who wish to do this as a career, this 24-day accredited qualification includes 2 practical assessment units and an online multiple choice examination.  This course will take you from absolute beginner to being ready to groom dogs for a living.

With our City & Guilds Level 2 qualification, you’ll learn all there is to know about dog grooming.  Both theory and practical elements make up this in-depth training course, which will equip you to start out in the world of dog grooming, either as an employee at a salon or starting your own grooming business.

Learning from Liza Smith, our founder who has been grooming for over 20 years, you’ll receive so much more than the course curriculum dictates. From running your own business and choosing your equipment, to advanced grooming techniques not typically covered under this qualification, Liza ensures that all of our C&G students are ready to hit the ground running when they graduate.

Our course content includes inputs on:

  • Dog handling
  • Coat types and breed types
  • Shampoos and product choices
  • Ectoparasites
  • Health checking and assessment
  • Methods of drying
  • Canine first aid
  • Health and safety consideration
  • Codes of practice
  • Restraining equipment
  • Maintenance of equipment and methods of waste disposal

In addition, you’ll also receive significant practical grooming experience and training, including pet trims, clipping different coat types and shapes, nail clipping, ear cleaning, scissoring and thinning.

Groom Your Own Dog

1 Day Course

Designed for dog owners who want to learn how to keep their own pet’s coat in good style and condition.  This 1-day course is specific to your pet’s breed and will give you some great simple techniques to enable you to groom your own dog to a good standard, including bathing, grooming, trimming and styling.

This is a fantastic one to one experience with Liza where she ensures that you get the optimum value from your day to ensure that you can confidently, with all the tools and techniques, continue to groom your own dog!

The course is held on a Saturday or Sunday between 9:30am and 4pm and we cover the following:

  • Health check
  • Correct bathing and preparation
  • Drying techniques
  • Scissor techniques
  • Nail trimming
  • Feet shape 
  • Body styling
  • Head styling
  • Equipment advice

It is an intensive one to one day but by the end of our training session not only will you know how to groom your dog well from start to finish, but you’ll also have material to take away with you – including photos and videos of you and your dog during the session to ensure that you have everything you need to continue grooming your own dog to a great standard!

Quick Dog Grooming Refresher

5 Day Course

Designed for working dog groomers and grooming assistants who want to upskill and refresh their grooming knowledge, this 5-day course will help improve your style and technique for a number of popular breeds.

In-depth Dog Grooming Refresher

10 Day Course

Designed for those with some background experience in dog grooming, this 10-day course will help improve your practical grooming ability, increase your repertoire of skills and techniques, and provide focus on specific breeds.
